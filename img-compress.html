Compression Tips

  • WebP format offers the best compression with quality preservation
  • Use 70-80% quality for optimal balance between size and quality
  • For web use, aim for images under 200KB
  • Resize images before compression for better results
  • Use lower compression for photos with text or fine details

Supported Formats

  • JPG/JPEG: Best for photographs and complex images
  • PNG: Best for images with transparency
  • WebP: Modern format with superior compression

Free Online Image Compression Tool

Our image compression tool helps you reduce the file size of your images without noticeable quality loss. This is essential for faster website loading times, saving storage space, and improving user experience.

With adjustable compression levels from 10% to 95%, you have full control over the balance between image quality and file size. The tool supports JPG, PNG, and WebP formats, allowing you to choose the best format for your needs.

Compressed images load faster on websites, improving SEO rankings and reducing bounce rates. Our tool is completely free to use, with no watermarks added to your images. All processing happens in your browser, ensuring your images remain private and secure.
